What is Musify?

A platform that lets you search for Events, Venue, City, State, Country, Date, and time. This is possible by searching your favorite artists, locations, and venues.

Who made it?

Scott, Jon, Quinton, and Sheriff

Why

Everyone loves music, so we wanted to make a tool for music lovers to find events going on around the world or just around the corner, by their favorite artist or at their favorite spot.

Thanks for coming!

Bio

We are a group of new coders from Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, and this is our first project. Creators: Scott, Jon, Sheriff, and Quinton.
